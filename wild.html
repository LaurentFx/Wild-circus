About Us

  • A little history...

    Our Circus was created in 1974, by 2 avant-garde artists, respectively clown and acrobat. They were joined little by little, over the years and representations. The team is today represented by more than 20 artists, who are waiting for you impatiently, to express their art and their talent.

  • The circus...

    His Tent heated and air-conditioned, is 42 meters in diameter, 30 meters high and can accommodate more than 3,000 people. His decorations were created and offered over time, by our fans and local artists, and traces our history, during its decades of existence.

  • The artists...

    Come young and old discover more than 2 hours of shows: acrobats, fire-eaters, gymnasts, trapeze artists, clowns, contortionists, dressage and taming, balancing, fakir, jugglers, magicians, ventriloquists, ...
